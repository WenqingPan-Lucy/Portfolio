Project Overview

The film aims to inspire viewers to rethink beauty standards, embracing the idea that "beauty lies in diversity, and value comes from within," encouraging everyone to make peace with themselves.

Artist Statement


The Gen Z lives in an era of information explosion, engaging with various social platforms and interacting with different people. These virtual interactions on the internet have both advantages and disadvantages. One of the most apparent drawbacks is the rise of appearance anxiety among some millennials. The majority of individuals portrayed online are often portrayed as attractive, creating a disparity with reality that can lead some young people to become insecure about their own appearance, eventually resorting to extreme cosmetic procedures. The underlying theme of this short film is to convey to those living with appearance anxiety that their appearance is their asset, and embracing their true selves is the most beautiful choice they can make.
