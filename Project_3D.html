Low Poly Virtual Pet Community

Project Overview

Aimed at creating a therapeutic interactive space where virtual pets provide emotional comfort to individuals who cannot own pets due to allergies or other constraints.

Artist Statement

This project is designed to investigate the feasibility of utilizing virtual pets as substitutes for real-life therapy dogs. The project involves the utilization of Blender to construct a modeldepicting an indoor dogplayroomandavirtual pet dog. Within the virtual environment, a virtual pet dog is introduced, providing users with interactive experiences guided by the virtual dogs behavior and expressive features. The overarching goal is to extend the joy typically derived fromreal dogstoindividuals who mayfaceconstraints in acquiring a physical pet, whether duetofinancial limitations or health considerations. This virtual pet serves as anaccessible alternative, fostering similar emotional connections and medical benefits
