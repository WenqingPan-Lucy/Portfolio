Chang Family Manor AR Recreation

Project Overview

This project combines narrative-driven gameplay with Augmented Reality (AR) technology to guide users through an immersive exploration of Changjia Manor, blending history, storytelling, and interactive experiences.

Artist Statement:


Our project is rooted in the desire to bring new life to Changjia Manor, a historic estate in Taiyuan that, despite its rich cultural heritage, has seen a decline in visitor numbers and public attention. The manor, with its centuries-old history, deserves to be appreciated and remembered, yet it remains largely overlooked by modern audiences. In response to this challenge, we have combined the innovative potential of Augmented Reality (AR) technology with narrative-driven gameplay to create an immersive experience designed to engage visitors and deepen their understanding of the manor’s significance. By integrating AR with storytelling, we have crafted a game that encourages players to explore the estate, triggering interactive narratives at specific locations. These narrative moments uncover the stories and historical context behind the manor, bringing its past to life in a way that is both educational and entertaining.

01. On-site Visit

To begin our project, we first visited Taiyuan and explored Changjia Manor, immersing ourselves in the local culture and history. This initial research phase allowed us to gain a deeper understanding of the region's heritage and the significance of the manor. By engaging with the community and the environment, we identified key cultural landmarks and unique features of the estate that could serve as interactive anchor points for our narrative-driven game.

02. Model Data Collection

We conducted on-site model data collection using tools such as the "3D Scanner APP" to capture 3D image data of key structures and features within Changjia Manor. This process allowed us to create accurate digital representations of important areas within the manor, preserving their details for integration into our augmented reality experience.

03. Literature Review


We conducted an extensive literature review to gain a deeper understanding of the historical and cultural significance of Changjia Manor. By researching historical documents, articles, and other relevant sources, we were able to uncover key events, figures, and cultural elements that have shaped the manor's legacy over the centuries.

04. Storyline Development


We crafted a compelling storyline rooted in mystery to guide players through an immersive exploration of Changjia Manor. The narrative is designed to captivate players by weaving suspenseful elements into the journey, encouraging them to uncover hidden clues and piece together the manor’s rich history. As players progress through the game, they encounter various cultural landmarks and artifacts, each revealing a fragment of the story and shedding light on the heritage of the Chang Family Manor. This approach not only engages players in an exciting adventure but also deepens their appreciation for the cultural and historical significance of the site.

05. Game Development


We utilized Unity and Blender to refine the collected 3D data and develop a functional game demo. Blender was used to clean, enhance, and optimize the scanned models, ensuring accuracy and performance for integration into the game. After refining the assets, Unity served as the primary platform for creating the game, enabling us to build an interactive and immersive demo that combines the refined 3D models with the narrative and AR elements.
