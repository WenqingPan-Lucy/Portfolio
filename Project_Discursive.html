Discursive Design--Grooming

Project Overview

This project explores children’s sex education awareness through provocative design

Artist Statement

This is a group project focusing on childrens sex education awareness. The project aims to raise awareness about childrens sex education. The project outcomes are mainly divided into three parts: a childrens book containing a large number of romantic hints, a teddy bear doll packaged with numerous sexual hints,and a box game on how to identify adults with pedophilic tendencies.

Magazine


The "Magazine" section is designed to mimic the appearance of children's books that could potentially be used as tools for grooming. It adopts pink as the primary color, a shade often favored by young girls, and includes age-inappropriate elements such as sexy toys and the promotion of unhealthy romantic ideals. This work aims to highlight the hidden risks in seemingly harmless materials and raise awareness about the importance of safeguarding children from such threats.

Grooming Bear


The "Grooming Bear" product serves as a critical exploration of how everyday objects, like toys, can be used to obscure harmful intentions under a guise of innocence. This teddy bear is packaged in a box designed to resemble a dollhouse, appealing to young girls with its hollowed-out front adorned with popular patterns and its interior styled like a girl's room. However, the room's decor incorporates numerous elements suggestive of precocious sexual themes. Additionally, the packaging features two bears—one large and one small—with a heart placed between them, subtly conveying inappropriate implications. The packaging also prominently features advertising slogans with subtle sexual innuendos, calling attention to how such seemingly benign products could potentially manipulate children. This design aims to provoke reflection on the hidden dangers in children's consumer goods and advocate for greater vigilance in safeguarding children's well-being.

Box Game


The "Box Game" is designed to educate children on how to recognize inappropriate behaviors and identify identify adults with pedophilic tendencies. Through a series of engaging scenarios and activities, the game encourages children to think critically about personal boundaries and safety in a playful yet informative way.
