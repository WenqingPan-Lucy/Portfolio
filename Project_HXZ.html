Han Xizai's Night Banquet

Project Overview

An LLM-powered interactive experience that allows users to converse with characters from The Night Revels of Han Xizai, uncovering its historical stories and cultural context.

Artist Statement:


This project reimagines the renowned Chinese painting The Night Revels of Han Xizai as an interactive experience powered by large language models (LLMs). By bringing the painting's characters to life, we aim to bridge the gap between historical art and contemporary audiences, allowing users to engage in dialogue with figures from the painting and immerse themselves in the cultural and historical narratives of the Five Dynasties and Ten Kingdoms period. Through this fusion of technology and art, we strive to deepen the appreciation of traditional Chinese culture, offering a dynamic and engaging way to explore the paintingâ€™s stories, symbolism, and the societal values it reflects. This work highlights the enduring relevance of historical art in the digital age, encouraging a dialogue between past and present.
