Nanjing-both old and new

Project Overview

A photography documentary capturing the transformation of Nanjing, a historic cultural city, from its rich past to its modern present.

Artist Statement


Nanjing, as the capital city of Jiangsu Province, boasts a rich history. It has served as a capital during both the Six Dynasties and the Ten Kingdoms period. Its abundant cultural heritage integrates both ancient and modern architectural styles. This documentary captures the changes in three areas of Nanjing Bianqianmen, Fuzimiao, and the Xuanwu District Health Station over the past few decades, even spanning over a hundred years. Through interviews with local elders, the documentary employs a narrative style that vividly conveys the audience through the vicissitudes of history, allowing them to feel the profound changes over time
